Frequently Asked Questions

My name is Austin Sundloff, but I go by Ausy online.

I am a hobbyist illustrator, comic writer, and programmer. Not professionally yet so don't think that.

What are your hobbies?

I enjoy creating digital art, writing comics, and programming.

Where do you study?

I am currently studying Software Engineering at Utah Tech University.

You can reach me through the contact page on this website, or emailing me at ausyozx@gmail.com
