Understanding my Audience

Location

I would imagine that someone who is interested in seeing my website would come from anywhere, but to be realistic, they would likely come from an English speaking region like the US, Canada, etc...

Age

My website will be completely family friendly, meaning that it will be open to all ages that would like to see it, however, it is still a portfollio, so those who are more likely to see it would be people from their late teens to possibly their 30s.

Gender

Gender isn't really a factor to consider. Since the website is about me and my work for professional reasons, any gender will be available to see it.

Other Demographics

The people who I would like to see my work the most are people who are interested in hiring me for work. I would like to have a professional piece of work under my belt that proves I am capable of working in the software field, and something like this would be a step in the right direction.
